Open Mic

Open Mic is a platform for fans of local comedy to rate and review local comedy shows and comedians. Comedians can use Open Mic to build a reputation and gain feedback from fans.

My first group project! TechStart is a place for employers and job seekers to meet. Companies place profiles on TechStart so that job hunters can find information about them more easily.
